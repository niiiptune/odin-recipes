Tomato and Egg

Tomato and Egg is a traditional Chinese cuisine which goes well with rice.

Ingredients:
  • Tomato
  • Egg
Steps:
  1. Stir the eggs
  2. Cook the eggs in a pan, then take them out once done
  3. Cook the tomatoes in the same pan until soft
  4. Throw the eggs back in, done!
